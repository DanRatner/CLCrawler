About

This web scrapper was created for users to be notified whenever a new listing of interest is added. I created this app when I was trying to buy my Miata, but I wanted anyone to be able to use it.

To use: Enter your email and data on what you are looking for. Currently this app is limited to the SF Bay craiglist listings. Enter your zip, max price, and max distance. You can also choose between scraping for cars or parts. The scrapper will continuously scan your search, and email you with any listings. You must keep the tab open for the scrapper to run
